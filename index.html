Who we are

Rich roots, faithful worship, and an open invitation.

Founded in 1968 in India, New Jerusalem Church continues to serve the spiritual needs of believers in Belgium while embracing a multicultural congregation.

Our mission is to share the Gospel of Jesus Christ, nurture spiritual growth, and serve people with love and compassion.
