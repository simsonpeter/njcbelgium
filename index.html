About Us

We are a vibrant community of believers dedicated to spreading the love of Christ through worship, service, and fellowship.

Our History

Founded in 1968 in India, we have served the spiritual needs of our community in Belgium for decades while embracing a multicultural congregation.

Our Mission

To share the transformative Gospel of Jesus Christ, nurture spiritual growth, and serve our community with love and compassion.

Our Services
