About us

A vibrant church family rooted in worship and service.

We are a vibrant community of believers dedicated to spreading the love of Christ through worship, service, and fellowship - welcoming everyone seeking spiritual growth and connection.

Who we are

Growing in faith together with open hearts and steady hope.

Founded in 1968 in India, New Jerusalem Church has served the spiritual needs of the community for decades while maintaining rich traditions and embracing a multicultural congregation in Belgium.

Our church life centers on Christ, prayer, fellowship, and service - creating space for worshippers of all ages to learn, belong, and be strengthened in faith.
