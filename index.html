About Us

We are a vibrant community of believers dedicated to spreading the love of Christ through worship, service, and fellowship. Our church family welcomes everyone seeking spiritual growth and connection.

Our History

Founded in 1968 in India, we have been serving the spiritual needs of our community in Belgium for decades, maintaining our rich traditions while embracing our multicultural congregation.

Our Mission

To share the transformative Gospel of Jesus Christ, nurture spiritual growth, and serve our community with love and compassion, following Christ's example.

Our Services

Sunday Worship

Join us every Sunday at 14:30 for our main worship service featuring inspiring messages, prayer, and uplifting music in a welcoming atmosphere.

Prayer Meetings

Every 2nd and 4th Saturday at 5:00 PM, we gather for powerful prayer sessions where we intercede for our church, community, and world.
