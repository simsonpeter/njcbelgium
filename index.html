About our community

Rooted in faith, built around people.

We are a vibrant community of believers dedicated to sharing the love of Christ through worship, service, and fellowship. Our church family welcomes everyone seeking spiritual growth and connection.

Our History

Founded in 1968 in India, we have been serving the spiritual needs of our community in Belgium for decades while honoring our heritage and embracing a multicultural congregation.

Our Mission

To share the transformative Gospel of Jesus Christ, nurture spiritual growth, and serve our community with love and compassion, following Christ's example.

What defines us

A church experience centered on worship, prayer, and belonging.

Whether you are visiting for the first time or looking for a church family, our prayer is that you encounter Christ and feel genuinely welcomed.

  • Warm fellowship for individuals and families
  • Regular worship services and prayer gatherings
  • Spiritual encouragement through preaching and music
Gather with us

Regular gatherings for worship and prayer.

Join our church family throughout the month for worship, prayer, and encouragement in a welcoming, faith-filled atmosphere.

Sunday Worship

Join us every Sunday at 14:30 for our main worship service featuring inspiring messages, prayer, and uplifting music in a welcoming atmosphere.

Prayer Meetings

Every 2nd and 4th Saturday at 5:00 PM, we gather for powerful prayer sessions where we intercede for our church, community, and world.

What to Expect

Expect a welcoming environment with heartfelt worship, biblical teaching, prayer, and a community eager to receive you.

  • Friendly atmosphere for visitors
  • Church life shaped by faith and fellowship
  • Ongoing spiritual encouragement for all ages
